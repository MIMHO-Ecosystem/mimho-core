# 💧 MIMHO Liquidity Strategy

## Overview

The MIMHO liquidity model is designed to avoid traditional launch risks such as:

- Over-injected liquidity  
- Immediate sell pressure  
- Artificial price stabilization  

Instead, MIMHO adopts a **modular and controlled liquidity architecture**, fully aligned with DAO governance.

---

## 🚀 Official Launch

- **Date:** April 20, 2026  
- **Time:** 16:20 BRT  
- **Network:** BNB Chain  
- **DEX:** PancakeSwap  

The launch follows a **low initial liquidity model**, enabling organic price discovery from the very first block.

---

## 🧠 Core Principles

### 1. Low Initial Float

Only **0.30% of total supply** was distributed during the Genesis Phase.

This ensures:

- Minimal early dilution  
- Reduced sell pressure  
- Stronger price discovery dynamics  

---

### 2. No Forced Liquidity

MIMHO does not rely on immediate large liquidity injections.

Instead:

- Liquidity grows progressively  
- Market participants define price  
- No artificial floor is imposed  

---

### 3. DAO-Controlled Expansion

Liquidity decisions are not centralized.

They are controlled by:

- MIMHO DAO  
- Voting Controller  
- On-chain governance mechanisms  

---

## ⚙️ Liquidity Modules

### 🔹 Liquidity Bootstrapper

The Bootstrapper contract holds a large reserve of MIMHO tokens.

Purpose:

- Provide future liquidity depth  
- Support expansion when needed  
- Avoid premature supply release  

This module acts as a **strategic reserve**, not an immediate liquidity dump.

---

### 🔹 Inject Liquidity

The Inject Liquidity contract is responsible for controlled LP creation.

Key characteristics:

- Requires authorization (Voting Controller / DAO)  
- Has cooldown between injections  
- Auto-disables after execution  
- Sends LP tokens directly to burn address  

This guarantees:

- No uncontrolled liquidity events  
- Permanent liquidity locking  
- Transparent and auditable injections  

---
