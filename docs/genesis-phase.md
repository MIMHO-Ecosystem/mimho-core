# 🚀 MIMHO Genesis Phase (Presale Results)

## Overview

The MIMHO Genesis Phase represents the initial on-chain distribution of the MIMHO token.

This phase was intentionally designed to prioritize **organic market formation**, avoiding excessive early concentration and artificial demand.

---

## 📊 Final Results

- Hard Cap: 150 BNB  
- Total Raised: 0.4434 BNB  
- Total Supply Distributed: 0.30%  
- Tokens Distributed: 295,600,000 MIMHO  

---

## 🧠 Distribution Model

The Genesis Phase followed a deterministic on-chain logic:

- **TGE Unlock:** 20% immediately available  
- **Vesting:** 80% distributed over time  
- **Release Rate:** 5% per week  

This ensures gradual circulation and reduces early market pressure.

---

## ⚙️ Strategic Design

The low participation level was not treated as a failure, but as a structural advantage.

Key outcomes:

- Minimal circulating supply at launch  
- Reduced early sell pressure  
- Higher dependency on organic demand  
- Fair entry opportunity post-launch  

---

## 🔥 Market Implications

With less than 1% of total supply distributed before liquidity:

- Price discovery happens naturally  
- Volatility is expected and accepted  
- Early holders have aligned incentives  
- No artificial inflation from presale dumping  

---

## 💧 Liquidity Strategy

Liquidity is managed through:

- Liquidity Bootstrapper (token reserve)
- Inject Liquidity (controlled LP creation)

This ensures:

- No forced liquidity injection
- DAO-controlled expansion
- Sustainable growth mechanics

---

## 🛡️ Transparency

All actions were executed on-chain:

- Presale contract  
- Vesting contract  
- Registry-based architecture  
- Events emitted to MIMHO Events Hub  

No off-chain adjustments or manual overrides were performed.

---

## 🧩 Conclusion

The Genesis Phase establishes the foundation of MIMHO as:

- A **fair-launch aligned protocol**
- A **low initial float asset**
- A **DAO-driven liquidity system**

This phase is complete.

The next stage is **market formation**.
