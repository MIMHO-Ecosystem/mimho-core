## 👥 For Non-Technical Readers (Plain Explanation)

The **MIMHO DAO Vesting contract** exists to ensure that the DAO’s token allocation is released **gradually and responsibly**, instead of entering the market all at once.

In the MIMHO ecosystem, the DAO controls a large allocation of tokens intended for:

- Ecosystem growth
- Governance incentives
- Strategic partnerships
- Community initiatives
- Protocol expansion

If these tokens were released instantly, it could create unnecessary market pressure.

The **DAO Vesting contract prevents this scenario** by enforcing a **structured and transparent release schedule**.

Instead of unlocking everything at once, tokens become available **progressively over time**, only after the DAO becomes active.

This ensures:

- Market stability
- Responsible treasury management
- Long-term ecosystem sustainability

## 🔑 What the DAO Vesting Contract Does

The DAO Vesting contract:

- Holds the **DAO token allocation**
- Locks the tokens until DAO activation
- Releases tokens **gradually over time**
- Allows withdrawals only according to the vesting schedule

The contract guarantees that the **200B DAO tokens cannot be dumped into the market at once**.

Instead, they become available **in controlled portions**.

The **MIMHO DAO Vesting system prevents these risks**.

## ⏳ Vesting Logic

The DAO Vesting contract follows a **time-based release model**.

Key rules:

- Tokens remain locked **until DAO activation**
- After activation, tokens unlock **progressively**
- Only the **vested portion** can be withdrawn
- The remaining tokens stay locked

This mechanism ensures a **predictable and transparent token supply schedule**.

## 🧭 Token Release Model

The vesting contract ensures that:

- Tokens are released **in percentages**
- Unlock events are **deterministic**
- Withdrawals are **restricted to the vested amount**

Example flow:

1. DAO becomes active
2. Vesting schedule begins
3. A percentage of tokens becomes claimable
4. DAO can withdraw only the unlocked portion
5. Remaining tokens stay locked

This protects the ecosystem from **sudden liquidity shocks**.
