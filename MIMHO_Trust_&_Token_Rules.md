MIMHO Trust & Token Rules

(English – Official Version)

What is MIMHO?

MIMHO is a memecoin built with real infrastructure, long-term vision, and strict rules defined from day one. The project was designed to grow sustainably, protect holders, and transition to full community governance through a DAO.

All core rules described below are public, transparent, and verifiable on-chain.


---

Token Supply

Total fixed supply: 1,000,000,000,000 MIMHO (1 trillion)

No minting. No inflation.


Initial Distribution

Founder: 5% (50B) – vested via smart contract

Pre-sale: 10% (100B)

Initial Liquidity (LP): 30% (300B)

DAO Treasury: 20% (200B)

Ecosystem & Future Incentives: 20% (200B)

Future CEX Listings Reserve: 15% (150B)



---

Pre-Sale Rules

Pre-sale allocation: 100B MIMHO

Vesting is 100% automatic via smart contract

20% unlocked at purchase

5% unlocked weekly

No manual intervention



Unsold Tokens

Any pre-sale tokens that are not sold will be 100% permanently burned, reducing the total supply and benefiting all holders.


---

Liquidity (LP)

Liquidity pool will be created at launch on PancakeSwap (MIMHO / WBNB)

90% of the LP will be locked forever using the MIMHO Locker

10% of the LP will be allocated to the founder wallet, exclusively for project continuity, technical needs, and future migrations

Liquidity will never be removed for personal benefit



---

Token Fees (Immutable)

Buy tax: 1%

Sell tax: 1.5%


From the sell tax, 0.5% is automatically distributed to:

Liquidity reinforcement

Continuous burn

Staking rewards


These fees are immutable and cannot be changed.


---

Vesting & Locks (Code Only)

All vesting and locks in the MIMHO ecosystem are enforced strictly by smart contracts:

Founder vesting

Pre-sale vesting

Ecosystem vesting

DAO-related releases (only after DAO activation)


There is zero human intervention in vesting or unlocks.


---

Staking

Staking will be activated approximately 10 days after launch

Rewards are funded by protocol mechanisms

Designed to reward long-term holders



---

DAO Governance

The DAO will be activated after the project reaches operational maturity

Until then, the protocol is managed responsibly to avoid premature governance risks


DAO Structure

The DAO includes operational roles with defined responsibilities:

President

Vice President

Treasurer

Two Mediators


Compensation

Paid exclusively from the DAO treasury monthly revenue

Percentages are predefined and transparent

Roles have no power to harm or drain the protocol

Elections and impeachment mechanisms are enforced by code



---

Ownership & Renouncement

The token contract will be renounced once the DAO is fully active

This ensures a safe transition from founder-led setup to community governance



---

Multichain Vision

MIMHO launches on BNB Chain, with architecture and roadmap prepared for future multichain expansion.


---

Education & Incentives

MIMHO includes the Quiz Academy, where holders can learn about the ecosystem and earn MIMHO rewards through educational cycles.


---

Final Note

MIMHO was designed to outlive hype cycles. Clear rules, locked liquidity, immutable fees, and transparent governance are the foundation of the project from day one.

O que é a MIMHO?

---

Supply do Token

Supply total fixo: 1.000.000.000.000 MIMHO (1 trilhão)

Distribuição Inicial

Regras da Pré-venda

Alocação da pré-venda: 100B MIMHO

20% liberados na compra

5% liberados semanalmente

Sem intervenção manual



Tokens Não Vendidos

Staking

Presidente

Vice-presidente
